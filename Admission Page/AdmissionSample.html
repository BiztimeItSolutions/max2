INSTRUCTIONS TO THE CANDIDATES SEEKING ADMISSION

  1. The application form should be correctly filled. Enclose 4 Colour PP size Photographs and send to the Principal,H.K.B.K. College of Engineering,Bangalore,Karnataka,India,with a Crossed demand Draft for Rs.500/-( Rupees Five Hundred Only) Drawn in favour of H.K.B.K. College of Engineering,Bangalore, towards Registration fee of application and with documents securely fastened to the application,so as to reach him on or before the due date. The registration fee is not refundable.

    • MINIMUM QUALIFICATION:   Two years Pre University Examination of the Karnataka Pre University Education Board or any other equivalent examination with a minimum of 45 percent marks on the aggregate on the science group i.e., Physics, Mathematics and Chemistry/Biology/Bio-Technology/Electronics/Computer Science in the Final Examination, with Engish as one of the Subject of study.
    • As per Government norms,the above qualified student should have taken CET/KRLMPCA - CET or any other Entrance Test approved by the Government of Karnataka.

  3. Students from other University / Board should produce the ELIGIBILTY CERTIFICATE from REGISTRAR,V.T.U.,Belgaum along with the application.

  4. Students coming from other Universities should also produce Migration Certificate at the time of adission.

  5. Applications with incomplete information are liable to be rejected.

  6. Application received after the due date will not be entertained.

  7. Only attested copies of the required certificated are to be enclosed.with the application and the Original Certificates should be produced at the time of Selection/Admission.

  8. Candidates called for interview,should appear before the selection committee,at their own cost.

  9. Candidates selected for admission would be intimated by ordinary post.


  10. The Management reserves the right to cancel the admission of a candidate at any stage, if and when is detected that admission is against the rules.
    Note:

  1. The branch once allotted will not be changed later.

  2. All admissions are subject to final approval of the Visvesvaraya Technological University(VTU) Belgaum and the Institute shall not be responsible for any loss to the candidate if the admission is not approved by the University. Principal H.K.B.K. College of Engineering
